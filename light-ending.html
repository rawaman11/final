Light Ending

Mio chooses the light path and finds herself in a sunlit clearing. She is greeted by a wise old owl, who tells her that she has passed the test of the Whispering Woods. The owl gives her a magical feather, which will protect her from harm and guide her on her journey.

As Mio leaves the Whispering Woods, she feels a sense of peace and clarity. She knows that she has the strength and courage to face whatever challenges lie ahead.

And so, Mio's adventure continues...
