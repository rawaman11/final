The Dark Ending

Mio chooses the dark path, descending into the shadows. She finds herself in a dark, twisted forest, where the trees whisper of ancient evils. She hears a voice calling her name, and follows it to a hidden cave.

Inside the cave, she finds a dark figure, cloaked in shadows. The figure offers her a choice: to join him in darkness, or to return to the light.

What will Mio choose? We might never know..
